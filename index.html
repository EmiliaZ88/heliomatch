FAQ

Frequently asked questions

These answers support trust, reduce friction, and keep the site aligned with your direct-link model.

Does HelioMatch collect my details?

No. The rebuilt site does not add lead forms, account creation, or quote request capture. The calculator runs in the browser and legal copy reflects that model.

Are the savings and payback estimates guaranteed?

No. They are indicative only and depend on factors like system size, region, tariff, orientation, self-consumption, and installer pricing.

Can I use this site on mobile?

Yes. The core layout, calculator panels, and comparison tables have been rebuilt to work cleanly on smaller screens.

Where do outbound affiliate clicks go?

Some links may take you directly to external provider websites. HelioMatch does not use quote forms or sell your enquiry details.

How does HelioMatch make money?

HelioMatch may earn commission from some provider links if a user clicks through and later buys. This does not turn HelioMatch into a quote form or lead generation site, and the site does not sell user enquiry data.

Do I need planning permission for solar panels in the UK?

Many domestic solar installations fall under permitted development, but exceptions can apply for listed buildings, conservation areas, flats, or unusual roof situations. Always check the current local rules if you are unsure.
