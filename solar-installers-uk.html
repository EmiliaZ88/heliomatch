Value lens

Compare entry price against scope

Cheaper quotes are only better value if system size, panel quality, monitoring, and aftercare are comparable.

Quality lens

Prioritize warranties and support

For higher-budget buyers, look at workmanship cover, battery support, and complaint-handling processes as closely as panel brand.

Service lens

Check the post-install journey

Good service is usually easier to spot in survey speed, handover documents, guarantees, and clear support routes than in headline ads.

Tariff lens

Match the system to usage

The right installer for a battery-heavy home can be different from the right installer for a simple cash-purchase solar-only setup.

Finance lens

Monthly options need scrutiny

Monthly-payment routes can reduce upfront spend but may materially change lifetime value, ownership, and cancellation rights.

Project scope

Residential and commercial differ

Homes, mixed-use sites, and commercial roofs often need different installers, survey methods, and finance structures.
